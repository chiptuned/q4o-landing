06 · FAQ

Frequently asked questions

Answers to the most common questions about the q4o model.

Who is actually doing the work, a robot?
Agents handle the heavy lifting: building, testing, documenting. Every decision and every delivery is reviewed by a senior engineer with 10+ years of experience. Think of it as a senior architect with a tireless team that never sleeps.
How are your prices usually lower than a big firm's?
We don't carry pyramid overhead. Every project runs with a senior engineer and a stack of agents that already know how to execute. You pay for shipped work, not ramp-up time. The difference comes from a leaner model, not from cutting corners.
What if the project doesn't work out?
Start with a 1-month pilot on a contained scope. No long-term contract. You keep everything we build: code, tests, documentation. If it works, scale up. If not, walk away with zero sunk cost beyond the pilot.
Is my data safe?
Yes. Our stack runs on Scaleway, a French cloud provider operating exclusively in France. Your code and data stay fully GDPR-compliant and never cross borders. We sign NDAs before any engagement, and you own 100% of what we produce.
I don't have a technical team. Can you still help?
Absolutely. Many of our clients are business leaders without in-house tech teams. You explain the problem in plain language. We handle everything from architecture to deployment. You get a working product, not a requirements document.

Tell us what you're trying to ship.

A 30-minute call. No jargon. We come back with a scoped proposal in 48 hours, free of charge.
