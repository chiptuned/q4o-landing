Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to the most common questions about the q4o model.

Are the AI agents fully autonomous?
No. Every agent is supervised by a senior human lead. AI handles the volume work (code generation, data pipeline scaffolding, testing, documentation), while humans handle architecture decisions, code review, and client communication.
How do you guarantee quality at 70% less?
Traditional ESNs bill for time, not output. Most junior consultant time goes to learning the stack, writing boilerplate, and sitting in meetings. Our AI agents eliminate that overhead. You pay for deliverables, not headcount.
What's the lean methodology angle?
Every project is broken down into value streams. We identify waste (waiting, context switching, rework) and configure agents to eliminate it. Short iterations, continuous delivery, no bloat.
Can I start with a small pilot?
Yes. Most clients start with a 1-month, 1-agent pilot on a contained scope. No long-term commitment required. If it works, scale. If not, stop.
What technologies do you cover?
Python, Rust, TypeScript, SQL, dbt, Spark, Airflow, Terraform, AWS/GCP/Azure, React, FastAPI, LangChain, and most modern data/AI stacks. Our agents are trained on your codebase and conventions.
