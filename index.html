Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to the most common questions about the q4o model.

Who's actually doing the work — a robot?
AI agents handle the heavy lifting — building, testing, documenting. But every decision and every delivery is reviewed by a senior engineer with 10+ years of experience. Think of it as a senior architect with a tireless team that never sleeps.
70% savings sounds too good to be true. What's the catch?
No catch — just a different model. Traditional firms bill for headcount and hours, including all the time juniors spend learning your business. Our agents already know how to execute. You pay for results, not ramp-up. The savings come from eliminating overhead, not cutting corners.
What if the project doesn't work out?
Start with a 1-month pilot on a contained scope. No long-term contract. You keep everything we build — all code, all documentation. If it works, scale up. If not, walk away with zero sunk cost beyond the pilot.
Is my data safe?
Yes. Our AI runs on Scaleway, a French cloud provider operating exclusively in France — your code and data stay fully GDPR-compliant and never cross borders. We sign NDAs before any engagement, and you own 100% of what we produce.
I don't have a technical team. Can you still help?
Absolutely. Many of our clients are business leaders without in-house tech teams. You explain the problem in plain language. We handle everything from architecture to deployment. You get a working product, not a requirements document.
